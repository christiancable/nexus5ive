Help Nexus

 

Anyone can help nexus if they want to and you don't even have to be a dull computer type!

Every once in a while someone wants or feels they want to do something "for nexus," here are a few ways you could do this.

1: Bring a friend

Nexus is a place for a whole bunch of people to chat, giggle and plan world domination. The more people posting to nexus the better it will be. Getting a friend to join and to take part is the most effective way of helping nexus. We have a page for new users to sign up [here]

2: Help improve the workings of nexus

Nexus is an open source application written in PHP using MySQL as the database backend and a whole bunch of templates written in plain old html. Anyone who can donate the time and knows even a small bit about PHP or creating web pages can help! If you are interested in this then please contact me and I'll give you all the help I can. You can view the current source code of nexus [here]

3: Donate a quid!

Nexus is hosted and run without advertising of any kind. The cost of this is a modest charge of about 6 pounds per month, all of which comes from my pocket. A pound is pretty much nothing these days and yet just a few people every once in a while donating a pound will easily reduce the cost of running nexus to nothing.
